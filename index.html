Bio

Hello, I’m Evan Chiu, first and foremost a follower of Jesus Christ. I’ve been a believer since the age of five, when I chose to follow Christ at a Children’s Church service. Time since then has been spent growing, learning what it is to be patient, kind, loving, when the actions don’t come naturally. Jesus Christ is a life changer, and I’d love to tell you about him.

I married my incredible wife, Jessi, on 1/1/11. Marriage is an epic journey. It starts out like a magical sleepover with your best friend. As you grow, you find various challenges of learning how to interact together, with old friends, with new friends. We’re both blessed to have amazing and supportive families.
